/bio

Set a short "about me" text that appears when others look you up with /whois.

Syntax

/bio <text>
/bio
/bio clear

Parameters

text — Your bio text (max 200 characters). Use clear to remove your bio.

How It Works

Your bio is displayed in the /whois output when other users look you up. Running /bio with no arguments shows your current bio. If your text exceeds 200 characters, it is automatically truncated with a warning. When no bio is set, the bio field simply doesn't appear in /whois output.

Rules & Limits

  • Maximum 200 characters
  • Registered users: bio is saved across sessions
  • Guests: bio lasts only for the current session

Examples

/bio Elixir enthusiast from Brazil
/bio
/bio clear
