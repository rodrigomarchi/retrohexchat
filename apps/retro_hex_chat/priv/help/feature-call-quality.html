Call Quality

Monitor and adjust connection quality during calls.

Quality Indicator

The 4 bars indicate connection quality:

  • 4 green bars — Excellent
  • 3 green bars — Good
  • 2 yellow bars — Fair
  • 1 red bar — Poor

Manual Quality Adjustment

Use the High (1.5 Mbps), Medium (500 Kbps), or Low (150 Kbps) buttons to adjust the bitrate manually.

Automatic Adaptation

WebRTC automatically adjusts the resolution and framerate based on network conditions.
