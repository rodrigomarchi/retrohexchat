Notification Center

The notification center is a dropdown panel showing recent notifications in reverse chronological order.

Opening

Click the bell icon in the toolbar. A badge on the bell shows the number of unread notifications.

Entries

Each entry shows the relative time (e.g., "2 min ago"), the sender, the channel, and a content preview. Click an entry to navigate to the relevant channel or PM.

Mark All as Read

Click "Mark all as read" to clear all notification entries, reset treebar badges, and clear the favicon badge.

Limits

The notification center stores up to 50 entries (FIFO). Entries are ephemeral — they do not persist across page reloads.
