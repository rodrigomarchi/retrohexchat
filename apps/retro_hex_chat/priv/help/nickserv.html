NickServ Overview

NickServ is the nickname registration service. It protects your nickname with a password so nobody else can use it.

How Registration Works

Registration happens automatically on the connect screen. When you first connect with a new nickname, you'll be prompted to choose a password. On future visits, you'll enter that password to log in.

You can also manage your registration from inside the chat using the /ns command.

Commands

/ns register <password>   — Register your current nickname (if not already registered)
/ns identify <password>   — Identify (log in) for this session
/ns info [nickname]        — Look up registration info
/ns ghost <nick>           — Disconnect a ghost session
/ns drop <password>        — Permanently unregister your nickname

Why Register?

  • Protect your nickname — nobody else can use it
  • Persist your settings (notify list, contacts, highlight words, nick colors) across sessions
  • Access channels that require registration (mode +R)

Important Notes

  • Nicknames are case sensitive — "Alice" and "alice" are different
  • Nicknames do not expire — once registered, they stay yours until you /ns drop
  • If you change your nick in chat to a registered name you don't own, you have 60 seconds to identify before being force-renamed
