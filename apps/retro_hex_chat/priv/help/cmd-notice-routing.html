/notice_routing

Choose where incoming notices from other users appear on your screen.

Syntax

/notice_routing [active|status|sender]

Parameters

active — Show notices in your currently active window (default).
status — Show notices in the Status tab.
sender — Show notices in the sender's PM window (opens one if needed).

How It Works

This controls the delivery of user-targeted notices (sent via /notice). Channel notices always go to the channel window regardless of this setting. Running /notice_routing with no arguments shows your current preference. The setting is saved across sessions for registered users.

Examples

/notice_routing
/notice_routing status
/notice_routing sender
/notice_routing active
