Video Call

Peer-to-peer video calls with full-screen remote video and a local video thumbnail.

Starting a Video Call

In the P2P session, click Video Call. The peer must accept.

Layout

The remote video occupies the main area. Your local video appears in the bottom-right corner.

Turn Camera Off/On

Click Turn Camera Off to pause the video. The peer will see a placeholder.

Picture-in-Picture

Click PiP to pop the remote video into a floating window that stays visible while navigating other tabs.

Upgrade from Audio to Video

During an audio call, click Add Video. The peer must accept before the camera is activated.
