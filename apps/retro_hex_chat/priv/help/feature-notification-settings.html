Notification Settings

Configure all notification preferences from Options > Notifications.

Global Toggles

Sound notifications — Enable/disable notification sounds.
Title flash — Enable/disable browser title flash.
Browser notifications — Enable native OS notifications. Click "Request Permission" to grant browser permission.
Privacy mode — Hide message content in toasts and browser notifications.
Do Not Disturb — Suppress all interruptions.

Trigger Rules

Choose which events trigger notifications: mentions, PMs, all channel messages, joins/leaves.

Per-Channel Levels

Set each channel to Normal, Mentions only, or Mute. PMs are locked to "Always" and cannot be muted. Channel settings are cleaned up when you leave a channel.

Persistence

Registered users' preferences persist in the database. Guest preferences are saved to localStorage.
