Connecting

To connect to RetroHexChat, enter a nickname on the connect screen and click Connect.

Nickname Rules

  • 1–16 characters long
  • Must start with a letter (a–z, A–Z) or a special character: [ ] \ ^ _ ` { | }
  • Cannot contain spaces
  • Case sensitive — "Alice" and "alice" are different nicknames

Registration Required

Every nickname must be registered before entering the chat. When you connect with a new nickname, you'll be asked to choose a password to register it. On future visits, you'll enter your password to log in.

Connection Flow

1. Enter your nickname and click Connect.

2a. New nickname — A registration form appears. Choose a password (minimum 5 characters) and confirm it. Click Register & Connect.

2b. Registered nickname — A password prompt appears. Enter your password and click Connect.

3. You join the chat already identified and land in #lobby.

Nick Expiration

Nicknames not used for 7 days are automatically freed. Connect regularly to keep your registration. See Nick Expiration for details.
