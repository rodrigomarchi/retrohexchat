Status Tab

The Status tab is the central observability point for your session. Every system event, command result, and error is mirrored here, so you never miss important information even if you are looking at a different channel.

What Appears in Status

System events: joins, parts, kicks, bans, mode changes, topic changes, nick changes, knocks.
Command results: /whois, /alias, /ignore, /perform, /autojoin, /invite, /kick, /ban, /mode, /timer output.
Errors: permission errors, rate limits, invalid commands, send failures.
Services: NickServ, ChanServ responses.
Notifications: notify list online/offline, CTCP requests and replies, wallops.

Click the Status tab in the tab bar or treebar to view it. Status data is session-only and is not persisted.
