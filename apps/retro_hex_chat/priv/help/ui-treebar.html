Treebar

The treebar is the left navigation pane showing your channels, PMs, and the notify list in a tree view.

Header

The treebar header shows the title "Conversations" and a collapse button (◀) to hide the panel. You can also toggle it via View > Toggle Treebar.

Features

Click a channel or PM to switch to it. Channels with unread messages appear bold. Channels with highlights flash. Private messages display an envelope icon (✉) prefix.

Sections

Channels and Private messages are grouped into collapsible sections with uppercase labels.
