Treebar

The treebar is the left navigation pane showing your channels, inline user list, and PMs in a tree view.

Header

The treebar header shows the title "Conversations" and a collapse button to hide the panel. You can also toggle it via the toolbar.

Features

Click a channel or PM to switch to it. Channels with unread messages appear bold. Channels with highlights flash.

Active Channel

The active channel is pinned to the top of the Channels section. Below it, the user list shows all users sorted by role (owner, operator, half-op, voiced, regular), with role icons and colors. The user count appears in parentheses next to the channel name.

User Interactions

Click a username to open the context menu. Double-click to open a private message.

Sections

Channels and Private messages are grouped into collapsible sections with uppercase labels.
