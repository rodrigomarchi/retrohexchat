/invite

Send a channel invitation to another user. This is required for invite-only (+i) channels where users cannot join on their own.

Syntax

/invite <nickname> [#channel]
/invite auto

Parameters

nickname — The user to invite.
#channel — Target channel (defaults to your current channel).
auto — Toggle whether you auto-join channels when you receive invitations.

How It Works

The invited user receives a popup dialog with options to Join or Decline the invitation. The invitation expires after 5 minutes if not acted upon. For invite-only (+i) channels, only channel operators can send invites. Using /invite auto toggles your personal preference for automatically joining channels when someone invites you.

Rules & Limits

  • You must be in the channel to invite someone to it
  • For +i channels, you must be a channel operator
  • Invitations expire after 5 minutes

Examples

/invite Alice
/invite Alice #private
/invite auto
