Private Messages

You can send private messages to other users that only the two of you can see.

Starting a Conversation

/query <nickname>

This opens a PM tab. Alternatively, send a single message:

/msg <nickname> <message>

Using the User List

Click a nickname in the conversations sidebar user list and select Query to open a PM tab. You can also double-click a nickname to open a PM directly.
