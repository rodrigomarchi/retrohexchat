Do Not Disturb

Do Not Disturb (DND) mode suppresses all audible and visual notifications while still accumulating unread badges.

Activating DND

Click the moon icon in the toolbar, or enable it in Options > Notifications.

What DND Suppresses

Toast popups, notification sounds, title flash, and browser notifications are all suppressed in DND mode.

What Still Works

Conversations unread badges and the favicon badge continue to accumulate. The notification center still records entries. When you disable DND, you can review everything you missed.

Persistence

DND state persists across page reloads for both registered users and guests (via localStorage).
