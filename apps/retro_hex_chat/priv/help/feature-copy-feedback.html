Copy & Settings Feedback

Brief toast notifications confirm copy and settings save operations.

Copy Confirmation

When you copy text from the chat (via context menu or keyboard shortcut), a "Copied!" toast appears briefly at the bottom-right and fades after 2 seconds.

Settings Confirmation

When you save settings (OK or Apply in Options), a "Settings saved" toast confirms the save.
