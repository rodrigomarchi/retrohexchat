Connect Authentication

When you connect with a registered nickname, RetroHexChat prompts for your password directly on the connection screen — no need to run /ns identify manually.

How It Works

1. Enter your registered nickname and click Connect.

2. A password prompt appears. Enter your NickServ password.

3. On success, you join the chat already identified — the 60-second timer is skipped.

Unregistered Nicknames

If the nickname is not registered, you connect immediately without a password step.
