Connect Authentication

RetroHexChat handles all authentication directly on the connection screen — no need to run /ns identify or /ns register manually.

Registered Nicknames

1. Enter your registered nickname and click Connect.

2. A password prompt appears. Enter your NickServ password.

3. On success, you join the chat already identified — the 60-second identify timer is skipped.

New Nicknames

1. Enter a nickname that isn't registered yet and click Connect.

2. A registration form appears with Password and Confirm password fields.

3. Choose a password (minimum 5 characters) and click Register & Connect.

4. Your nickname is registered and you join the chat already identified.

Password Rules

  • Minimum 5 characters, maximum 100 characters
  • Stored securely with bcrypt hashing — never in plain text
